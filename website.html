Website Design

Story Of ITB, Website Design Assignment

This website assignment was about re-creating my college website because we were going to become the first IT University in Ireland. We were asked to make a website based on ITB in the past and now. On this website, you would find what and how ITB became an college, the history before the area became a college campus and the ITB library.


Fashion Inc, 2nd Year Group Project

This was our Second year Group Project website. This was our 'Fashion Inc.' website. On this website, you would find a history of different fashion style in the past, all the interviews we done and a fashion spinning wheel. You may have technical difficulties with the spinning wheel, it only works on a Mac or PC. We still couldn't figure out what went wrong.


Monster City, Parallax Website Assignment

This was our group parallax assignment for Web Design in second year. There was an Smarter Travel competition on at the time and we got the opportunity to sign up for it. We had to design a piece to encourage staff and students to choose smarter modes of transport, my group decided to do it on walking.


Electric Picnic, Website Assignment

This was a re-creation of the Electric Picnic festival website. We could design a different genre music and layout than the offical Electric Picnic webpage. I did it based on 'Pop Rock' music genre. To make it look like it was 'Pop Rock', typography and colours are important. Which is why the font and colour matches the genre.


Tiny Farm, Multimedia Assignment

This wasn't really a website. We didn't use Adobe Dreamweaver to design this assignment. We used Adobe Animate, this was a group assignment for Multimedia. We could do any project we will but we decided to make a game for children from the age of 4-6. An education maths game for children in primary school. If page doesn't load for you, it's because you need 'FLASH Player'
