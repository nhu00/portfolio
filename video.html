Alvin Pascal, 2nd Year Group Project Interview

This interview was for our Group Project assignment in second year. We asked one of my friend to be in the video for us, I asked Alvin because I know he loves fashion and is always keeping up with the trends. Alvin’s style is a reflection of his taste in music. He credits Travis Scott as one of his style influencers along with Tyler the creator and Kanye wets, he believes they have made a big change to his life and given him the confidence to wear the fashion he does now.


Carlos Cleto, 2nd Year Group
Project Interview

This interview was also for our Group Project assignment in second year. We asked my cousin's friend to be in the video for us because I knew he has a certain style he wore in the past and now. Carlos believes his style is reflection of who he is and how he wants to be portrayed. He takes inspiration from a mix of decades but mostly from the nineties skater scene with touches of new brands and trends. He describes his style as a mix of casual street style.


Alina and Adina Toader, 2nd Year
Group Project Interview

This was another interview for our Group Project assignment in second year. We asked these twins because I knew they been wearing the same style since they were kids. They describe their style as trendy, casual but with added personality with the use of accessories. They use fashion as a way to express themselves, fashion is a key part of their lives it’s a way for them to tell the world who they are and how close of a bond they have together.
